The Atelier

Founded in 2021 • Based in a quiet mountain studio

LUNARIS was born from a desire to capture the beauty found only in darkness. Every painting is created by hand in our private atelier using centuries-old techniques fused with contemporary vision.

We believe art should live, breathe, and evoke emotion. Whether you choose an existing work or commission a bespoke piece, you become part of a legacy of collectors who value depth over decoration.
